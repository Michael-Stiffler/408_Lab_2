Story of my Cat Rocket

Title description, Feb 13, 2022

Above is a picture of my cat Rocket

Rocket is a special cat to me because he is a rescue. One night in the middle of Septeber 2020, Rocket cried outside of my house as if he was in pain. When I went out to check what the noise was, I was surprised to see a cat making such a loud and disturbing sound. After giving rocket some food, he became super friendly with me and let me pet him. Later that night he followed me inside and slept by my side. I thought he would eventually want to go back out, but rocket refused to even go near the door and has been completely okay with staying with me. Since then Rocket has gotten all his shots and has become super healthy. He is very sweet and doesn't understand the concept of personal space. The doctor believes he is 5 years old.
